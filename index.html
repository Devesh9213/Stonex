About Tri StoneX Concepts

Crafting Excellence in Stone

New Delhi's Premier Stone Crafters

We are specialists in CNC-designed marble using state-of-the-art Italian machinery. Our expertise lies in creating intricate engravings and bespoke stonework that transforms spaces into works of art.

Working with both Indian and imported marble, we serve residential, commercial, and architectural clients worldwide. Our commitment to precision and quality has made us a trusted name in stone craftsmanship.
